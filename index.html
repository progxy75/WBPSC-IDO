WBPSC IDO Recruitment Examination 2023: Application Process

1. Click on the Application Link

2. After that log in with your User ID and Password, if you are not a new user. Otherwise first sign up with the official portal.

4. Then select advertisement no 10/2023.

5. Enter all of your educational credentials and other information.

6. Verify all of the information you’ve entered and then submit the form.

7. When finished, print the WBPSC IDO Recruitment application form 2023 for future reference.

WBPSC IDO Recruitment Examination 2023: Application Fee

Candidates have to pay an application fee of Rs. 160/- (one hundred and sixty rupees).

 Furthermore, candidates will be required to pay the following:

  • For online payments using debit/credit cards, the convenience fee is 1% of the examination fee, with a minimum of Rs. 5/- (Rupees Five) + 18% G.S.T.
  • Convenience cost of Rs. 5/- (Rupees Five) plus 18% G.S.T. on convenience fee for online payments via net banking.
  • Service charge of Rs. 20/- (Rupees Twenty) applies to offline payments made at a bank counter.

S.C. /S.T. candidates from West Bengal, as well as persons with disabilities (P.W.D.) with a physical disability of 40% or greater, are exempt from paying any fees. West Bengal BC candidates, on the other hand, must pay the standard price.

WBPSC IDO Recruitment Examination 2023: Eligibility Criteria

A candidate must meet the eligibility requirement as set by WBPSC in order to sit for the IDO Recruitment Examination.

a) Educational Qualification

A university degree or a diploma in engineering or technology from a qualified university/institution.

b) Age Criteria

21 – 39 Years

Applicants belonging to Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribes, and Other Backward Classes (Non Creamy Layer) may be exempted from age restrictions as per Government regulations.

WBPSC IDO Recruitment 2023: Exam Pattern

Candidates can review previous WBPSC IDO exam question papers to acquire a sense of the exam pattern. It is important for candidates to understand the exam pattern so that they may prepare appropriately. There is no major change in the exam pattern from year to year. The WBPSC IDO Exam Pattern is as follows:

  • The exam has a total of 100 marks.
  • Each question carries 1 mark
  • The duration of the exam is 1 hour and 30 minutes 
  • There is no negative marking

WBPSC IDO Recruitment Examination 2023: Interview

The interview  is primarily a stage in which candidates are asked questions on many topics including IQ based question like any other interview. Candidates are chosen based on their merits.

WBPSC IDO Recruitment Examination 2023: Selection Process

The Public Service Commission will conduct the IDO Recruitment Examination  in order to fill 118 IDO vacancies. The selection process involves Screening Examination (Written Exam) and Interview.

WBPSC IDO Recruitment 2023: Salary Details

Candidates who will be selected can expect a basic salary package that falls within the range of Rs. 7,100 to Rs. 37,600 including other benefits.
